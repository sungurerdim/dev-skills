# Compliance Templates

Reusable legal and privacy compliance templates for app developers. All templates use `{PLACEHOLDER}` syntax for customization.

> **Disclaimer:** These templates are starting points, not legal advice. Consult with a legal professional for your specific jurisdiction and use case.

## Templates

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [DPIA Template](dpia-template.md) | Data Protection Impact Assessment — risk assessment for data processing |
| [Privacy Policy Template](privacy-policy-template.md) | User-facing privacy policy covering GDPR, CCPA, KVKK |
| [Breach Notification Template](breach-notification-template.md) | Incident response plan with regulatory timelines |
| [Processor Registry Template](processor-registry-template.md) | Third-party sub-processor documentation (GDPR Art. 28) |
| [Store Privacy Labels Template](store-privacy-labels-template.md) | Apple/Google store privacy declarations guide |
| [Legal Checklist](legal-checklist.md) | Comprehensive legal compliance checklist |

## Usage

1. Copy the template you need
2. Replace all `{PLACEHOLDER}` values with your app's specifics
3. Have a legal professional review before publishing
4. Set a review schedule (recommended: every 6-12 months)
